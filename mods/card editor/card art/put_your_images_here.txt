Put your custom card portrait images in this folder.

- Supported: .png / .jpg / .jpeg / .webp / .gif

NORMAL portrait (Full Art OFF):
  - Aspect ratio: landscape 4:3 (wider than tall)
  - Recommended size: 1000x760
  - Best for: purpose-made card art, square-ish images
  - Wallpapers / wide images will be heavily cropped

FULL ART (Full Art ON, Ancient card layout):
  - Aspect ratio: portrait 3:4 (taller than wide)
  - Recommended size: 760x1000 or taller
  - Best for: wallpapers and wide images (they fill the whole card face)
  - Landscape wallpapers will be cropped on the sides

After adding files:
Creator -> Art dropdown -> pick "Custom: <filename>"
